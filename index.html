Monster Survivors

Fight endless waves of monsters, upgrade your character, and survive as long as possible!

Use WASD or arrow keys to move, and defeat as many monsters as possible!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike game inspired by popular titles like Vampire Survivors. Take control of a brave hero and face endless waves of monsters in a battle for survival!

As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience and unlock powerful upgrades that enhance your abilities. Choose your upgrades wisely to create synergistic builds that can handle the increasingly difficult monster waves.

How long can you survive against the relentless monster hordes? Challenge your friends to beat your high score and climb to the top of the leaderboards!

Game Features:

  • Roguelike gameplay with procedurally generated enemy waves
  • Unlock and upgrade various abilities and weapons
  • Multiple character classes with unique starting abilities
  • Epic boss battles that test your skills and strategy
  • Beautifully crafted pixel art graphics
  • Engaging sound effects and music

How to Play

Controls:

  • W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Space: Special ability (when available)
  • ESC: Pause the game

Tips for Success:

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Prioritize upgrading your favorite weapons first
  • Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters
  • Pay attention to enemy patterns and adapt your strategy

Game Progression:

  1. Start with basic abilities and defeat monsters to gain experience
  2. Level up to unlock new upgrades and enhance existing ones
  3. Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters and mini-bosses
  4. Collect power-ups and special items to boost your character
  5. Survive as long as possible to achieve a high score
  6. Game ends when your health reaches zero

Character Classes

Warrior

A formidable fighter with high health and strong melee attacks. The Warrior excels at close combat and can withstand significant damage.

Starting weapons: Sword, Shield Bash

Mage

A master of magical arts with powerful area-of-effect spells. The Mage can deal massive damage to groups of enemies from a distance.

Starting weapons: Magic Bolt, Frost Nova

Rogue

A nimble fighter with high evasion and critical hit chance. The Rogue excels at hit-and-run tactics and dealing burst damage.

Starting weapons: Dagger, Throwing Knives
